Invoiced Customer Reference

Invoiced Customer Reference is a plugin to display the customer reference in invoices. If you sell services without quantity management, this module becomes absolutely vital for you. Invoiced Customer Reference will help you to manage your quotations and your invoicing while keeping an eye on your customer reference in Odoo, and this until the printing of the report. Perform various operations, from printing a single invoice to merging several quotes on a single invoice, while keeping the customer reference on each line in case it varies between products.
